About Us

What We Offer

Welcome to our Mario Strikers community!

Here you can meet other fans, talk about everything related to the series, find regular matches, and join events and tournaments across all Mario Strikers games.

We also run the Mario Strikers League, where players compete to climb the ranks and become one of the greatest Strikers of all time.

Whether you're here to play casually, improve your skills, or compete at the highest level — you're welcome to join the fun!
