Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data Mario Strikers Community collects, how we use it, and your rights. Questions? Reach out via our Discord.

What We Display Publicly

  • Usernames — shown on leaderboards and player profiles. Your username may be your real name; please be mindful when choosing it.
  • Country flag — shown if you choose to set one. Entirely optional.
  • Competitive stats — ratings, match records, and tournament results linked to your username.

What We Collect Internally

Beyond publicly visible data, we may process the following for security and operational purposes:

  • Your IP address and basic browser information — never publicly visible. Used solely to detect and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Why We Process Your Data

  • To operate leaderboards, player profiles, and community features.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, cheating, and ban circumvention.
  • To maintain accurate historical tournament and rating records.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the EU/EEA and UK, we process data under:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — necessary to provide the service.
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in maintaining security and community integrity.

Data Retention & Deletion

You may request removal of your data at any time via Discord. We reserve the right to retain:

  • Data necessary to enforce bans and prevent circumvention.
  • Historical tournament results and rating records, which form part of shared community history.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us via Discord to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use only strictly necessary cookies for session management and security. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the current version.
